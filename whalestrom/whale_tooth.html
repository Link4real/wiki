Whale Tooth

General Information:

The Whale Tooth can by dropped by a Norhval.

It can be thrown, but can only damage other players and Norhvals.

It deals 1.5 hearts of damage.

It can be stacked to 16.

Sounds:

The Whale Tooth has a custom throwing sound.
